## Brewing Styles
I like to brew my coffee in **two ways**:
1. **Hot Aeropress Coffee**  
2. **Iced Aeropress Coffee**  

Both use the **Inverted Aeropress Method**, but the difference lies in the amount of water and whether ice is added.
